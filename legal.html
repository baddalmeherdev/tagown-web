Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026
Welcome to Tagown, operated by Meherwebstudio ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This extensive Privacy Policy governs the data collection, usage, and protection protocols for the Tagown Mobile Application (Android/iOS) and the Tagown Web Dashboard.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with our vehicle security and alert ecosystem, we must collect certain information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Your Full Name, Mobile Number, and secure Password. We verify mobile numbers using OTP via Firebase Authentication to ensure authenticity.
  • Vehicle Information: Registration Number (IND), Vehicle Type, Brand, Model, and optional vehicle photographs.
  • Emergency Profile (Optional): Age, Gender, Blood Group, and Trusted Emergency Contact details (Name and Phone numbers).

2. Explicit Use of Camera and Permissions

App Store and Play Store policies require strict transparency regarding hardware permissions:

Camera Usage: The Tagown platform requires camera access strictly for the purpose of scanning Tagown QR code stickers. We process the QR code locally on your device. We do NOT record videos, take background photos, or upload your live camera feed to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your information for purposes based on legitimate business interests, including:

  • Facilitating Communication: To allow third parties to contact you regarding your parked vehicle without exposing your actual mobile number directly on the vehicle.
  • Emergency Response: To display your emergency contacts to authorized personnel or first responders if your vehicle is involved in an incident.
  • Account Management: To secure your Document Vault and manage your linked Tagown QR tags.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal or medical data to advertisers. We may share information only in the following situations:

  • Consent: When you explicitly allow us to share details (e.g., displaying emergency numbers when QR is scanned).
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, court order, or police investigation to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users.

5. Data Security & Storage

We implement military-grade security measures. All data is securely encrypted and stored on Google Firebase servers. The "Document Vault" feature within our Mobile App utilizes local device encryption methodologies to protect your sensitive documents (RC, License).

6. User Rights & Account Deletion (Crucial)

You maintain full control over your data. You may review, change, or terminate your account at any time.

Data Deletion: You can instantly delete all your data by navigating to Settings & Profile > Delete Account Permanently. Executing this action will instantly wipe your Personal Info, Vehicle Data, and Emergency Contacts from our Firebase databases.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this notice, you may email our Grievance Officer at support@tagown.online or contact us via WhatsApp at +91-6372980014.
